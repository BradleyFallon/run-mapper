Body

RouteScout is planning-first on web. The interface should stay calm even when it is showing dense route information.

Body copy should be compact and readable. It exists to support decision-making, not to dominate the screen.

Caption text is for metadata, secondary notes, and subtle labels.

Cards

Scout Specs and Routes use different card logic

Scout Specs are plan cards. They should focus on priorities, parameters, and defaults. Route Cards are collectible-feeling outputs. They should feel more like objects, with route-specific stats, features, and stronger identity.
