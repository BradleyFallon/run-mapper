# Persona: Sam

## Snapshot

Sam is a trail runner who wants novelty and nature without giving up navigational confidence.

## Background

- age range: late 20s to late 30s
- enjoys trail running and outdoor variety
- open to adventure, but not recklessness
- wants help understanding whether a trail route is manageable

## Primary Motivation

She wants new trail routes that feel exciting while staying within her terrain comfort zone.

## What She Values

- trail character
- natural scenery
- manageable steepness
- navigational confidence
- lower hazard exposure

## Pain Points

- getting lost on unfamiliar trails
- unexpectedly steep terrain
- muddy or unstable surfaces
- route suggestions that overpromise and under-explain

## Product Fit

Likely plan category:
- `Trail`

Likely starter plan:
- `Trail Confidence Loop`

What RouteScout should do for her:
- generate trail-heavy options with guardrails
- interpret terrain clearly
- explain hazard-related tradeoffs
- make adventure feel guided rather than random

## Success Criteria

Sam should feel excited about the route before the run and not anxious about ending up on something
beyond what she intended.
