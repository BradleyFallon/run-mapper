# Persona: Flo

## Snapshot

Flo is a serious runner who treats route planning as part of training, not just a convenience.

## Background

- age range: early 30s to mid 40s
- trains consistently for races
- cares about workout specificity
- notices when a route misses the intended training effect

## Primary Motivation

He wants routes that are technically useful and repeatable for specific workouts.

## What He Values

- precision
- repeatability
- clean pavement
- reliable elevation patterns
- low interruption
- control over planning variables

## Pain Points

- routes with the wrong climb profile
- unexpected stops and crosswalks
- uneven surfaces that disrupt workouts
- generic route recommendations that ignore training intent

## Product Fit

Likely plan category:
- `Training`

Likely starter plan:
- `Race Prep Hills`

Likely saved plan behavior:
- create multiple saved plan variants for different workouts

What RouteScout should do for him:
- support detailed plan tuning
- show strong route metrics
- make plan duplication and variant management easy
- behave like a real training tool

## Success Criteria

Flo should be able to build a small library of saved plans that map cleanly to different workout
types and trust the resulting routes.
