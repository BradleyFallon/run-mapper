# Persona: Vee

## Snapshot

Vee is a business traveler who wants a reliable run in an unfamiliar place without having to think
too hard about route planning.

## Background

- age range: early 30s to early 40s
- travels regularly for work
- usually runs in the morning
- often staying in hotels near conference centers or business districts
- uses running as a way to feel grounded while traveling

## Primary Motivation

She wants a route she can trust quickly so she can get outside, clear her head, and be back on
time.

## What She Values

- confidence
- predictability
- low mental overhead
- easy navigation
- a route that feels safe enough
- pavement and stable footing

## Pain Points

- unfamiliar streets
- not knowing which areas feel safe
- early morning darkness
- limited time before work obligations
- worrying about getting turned around

## Product Fit

Likely plan category:
- `Confidence`

Likely starter plan:
- `Safe Early Morning`

What RouteScout should do for her:
- recommend a strong default plan
- keep the workflow simple
- emphasize route clarity and start proximity
- explain why the route is trustworthy

## Success Criteria

Vee should be able to open the app, make one or two edits, generate a route, and head out without
second-guessing it.
