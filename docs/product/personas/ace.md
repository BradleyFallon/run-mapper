# Persona: Ace

## Snapshot

Ace is a vacation runner who uses running as a way to explore a city rather than just log miles.

## Background

- age range: late 20s to late 30s
- enjoys city travel and local discovery
- likes memorable runs more than optimized workouts when traveling
- wants the run to feel like part of the trip

## Primary Motivation

He wants a route that helps him discover places worth seeing and makes the city feel more vivid.

## What He Values

- discovery
- interesting landmarks
- scenic variety
- neighborhood character
- coherent route storytelling

## Pain Points

- generic hotel-area runs
- missing the best parts of a city
- wandering without structure
- sightseeing routes that are awkward to actually run

## Product Fit

Likely plan category:
- `Explore`

Likely starter plan:
- `City Landmark Run`

What RouteScout should do for him:
- surface compelling highlights
- let him rank or select what matters
- generate routes that feel curated
- explain what makes each route worth doing

## Success Criteria

Ace should finish the run feeling like he explored the city in a way he would not have managed on
his own.
