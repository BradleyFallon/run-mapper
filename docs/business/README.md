## Document Set

- [deck/index.html](./deck/index.html)
  Browser-based founder deck covering the plan, brand, app design, timeline, marketing,
  budget, business model, exit logic, and founder ask.
- [routescout-one-page-brief.md](./routescout-one-page-brief.md)
  One-page founder-facing summary of what RouteScout is, why it matters, the near-term plan, and
  the funding ask.
- [business-plan.md](./business-plan.md)
  Business framing, product strategy, market focus, pricing direction, and exit logic.
- [timeline-and-budget.md](./timeline-and-budget.md)
  Proposed execution phases, delivery milestones, and budget for the next 3 months.
- [founder-compensation-proposal.md](./founder-compensation-proposal.md)
  Proposed developer pay structure, developer bonuses, and founder role split.
- [go-to-market-and-growth.md](./go-to-market-and-growth.md)
  Recommended launch sequencing, pricing, beta structure, and early growth proposal.
- [funding-through-1000-subscribers.md](./funding-through-1000-subscribers.md)
  Recommended total funding ask from now through release and 1,000 activated users.
- [detailed-budget-through-1000-users.md](./detailed-budget-through-1000-users.md)
  Line-item budget covering compensation, tools, infrastructure, ads, creative production, and
  support work through early traction.
- [six-month-spend-timeline.md](./six-month-spend-timeline.md)
  Month-by-month spending plan for the first 6 months, with month-6 framed as readiness for
  strategic conversations rather than a guaranteed sale.
- [customer-acquisition-funnel.md](./customer-acquisition-funnel.md)
  Funnel-tree view of how users move from awareness to planning, registration, app handoff, trial,
  and subscription.
- [us-iphone-runner-market-and-acquisition-plan.md](./us-iphone-runner-market-and-acquisition-plan.md)
  U.S. iPhone-runner TAM estimate, core demographic sizing assumptions, acquisition channels, and
  six-month iPhone subscription goals.
- [subscription-tier-strategy.md](./subscription-tier-strategy.md)
  Recommended Free / Plus / Pro structure, with free optimized for habit and Plus optimized for
  planning depth and conversion.
- [founder-term-sheet.md](./founder-term-sheet.md)
  Negotiation-ready founder term sheet covering funding, compensation, and conditional IP
  assignment.
- [founder-agreement-outline.md](./founder-agreement-outline.md)
  Section-by-section outline for the written founder/build agreement.
- [founder-proposal-summary.md](./founder-proposal-summary.md)
  Short founder-facing memo for the actual meeting.
