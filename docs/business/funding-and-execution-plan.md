# RouteScout Funding And Execution Plan

## Purpose

This document is the consolidated build, budget, and funding plan for RouteScout.

It combines the 12-week build proposal, the six-month operating plan, the line-item budget, and
the funding ask into one active reference.

## Core Recommendation

Recommended full funding commitment:
- `$42,000`

Recommended minimum initial deposit:
- `$15,000`

The key framing is:
- `$42,000` is the real funding ask
- `$15,000` is only the first deposit required to begin cleanly

## What The Plan Covers

This funding path is intended to carry RouteScout through:
- the web planner MVP
- the iPhone MVP
- early launch
- early growth
- `1,000` activated users

## Build Window

### Initial 12-Week Build Phase

Primary delivery sequence:
1. ship the web planner MVP
2. refine from early feedback
3. build and release the iPhone MVP
4. soft-launch and begin early activation

### Six-Month Strategic Window

Month 6 should be treated as the point where RouteScout is:
- launched
- branded credibly
- showing real traction
- ready for active partnership or acquisition outreach

This is a strategic readiness target, not a guaranteed exit date.

## Funding Structure

### Preferred Structure

- full `$42,000` funded up front into the LLC

### Acceptable Fallback

- `$15,000` funded immediately
- written commitment for the remaining `$27,000`
- release of remaining funds on a defined schedule or milestone basis

## Why The Ask Should Be The Full `$42,000`

This is the correct ask because:
- the scope is real and cross-functional
- the developer pay is already modest
- the plan includes actual launch and growth costs
- underfunding creates execution risk and leverage problems
- a founder who keeps `50%` primarily for financing should finance the actual plan

## What Success Should Look Like By Month 6

By month 6, RouteScout should aim to have:
- shipped web planner
- shipped iPhone product
- working billing
- early activated users
- early paying customers
- strong brand presentation
- credible user proof
- active outreach to a major sports or fitness brand

## Bottom Line

The clean founder proposal is:

1. approve the `12-week` build phase
2. approve `$1,000/week` developer pay
3. approve developer bonuses at `100` and `1,000` activated users
4. commit the full `$42,000` funding plan
5. fund at least `$15,000` immediately
6. treat month 6 as a strategic readiness target for serious outreach
